Tap on Larry for a random
image and a random quote!



Based on "Larry Pops Up!"Info

"Larry Pops Up! is a tiny program written by my friend Don Munsil (who also was lead programmer and helped design LSL7: Love for Sail!) to play random WAVe files after a user-defined period of inactivity (i.e., if you don't type or move your mouse for 10 minutes, it pipes up!). It will play any and all wav files that you place in its directory. It even comes with a few Larry lines to get you started."
